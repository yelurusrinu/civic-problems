Want to make India Better?

How can an ordinary person make India a better country? This very thought has provoked us to develop this website.

Problems

Now a days there are problems everywhere in India. For examples, traffic is increasing day by day. There are many accidents in this year compared to that of previous year because of the increasing traffic. Another examples would be of roadside garbage, improper roads - roads with potholes, corruption etc. Some of the problems are known to add but some are mysterious and no one cares about it untill some one points at it.

Solution

Our friends at Times Of India has given us an opportunity to bring such civic problems to the light just by voting on Better India website. We need to just create a issue in the website and if the number of votes crosses hundred, they will publish the issue on the newspaper and bring them to the people.

Steps

  • Register on Better India Website
  • Login to the website
  • Add an issue and provide the required details
  • Once the issue reaches 100 votes it would be published on the newspaper
